Kitchen gardens

Nature always demonstrates its elegance and sophistication
by creating pieces of art indeed.

There are many people with a big heart, professionals who work in our gardens, and desperately invest their kindred work in the arms of nature. Thanks to them, we have the freshest vegetables and fruits in our restaurants. Thank them very much!

Kazakhstan

Gardens are found in many parts of the world. More than four hectares of land are cultivated on the fertile lands of our Ukraine.

Territorie

Chernozem is a highly fertile soil layer, mostly black and has a pronounced granular - lumpy structure. This type of soil is best suited for use in agriculture and is often used in agricultural work.

These characteristics make our Territorie in Ukraine ideal for our 100% natural approach to growing.

Vegetables

Many vegetables are planted in our gardens, including mushrooms,оnions, beetroot, turnips, etc.

For planting, we use the highest quality grains, and do not use any chemicals.

Territorie

We have solid ground. Lightweight and permeable, needs regular watering. We also use home compost to enrich the soil with minerals.

Vegetable leftovers, slices and all the waste we receive are sorted and then delivered to the factories. In this way we pollute nature less and also benefit from it.

Vegetables

Thanks to nutritious soils, we get fresh vegetables that taste great and are large. In summer, on the our plantations you can also see watermelons, tomatoes, turnips, etc.
